FAQ

Questions about working with Ofetech

If you don’t see your question here, include it when you request a review and we’ll cover it in our reply.

Do you only work with certain cloud providers?

No. Ofetech works with on-prem environments and cloud platforms including AWS (RDS, Aurora, EC2), Azure, and GCP (Cloud SQL, Compute). The important part is understanding how your data is stored, accessed, and backed up—not forcing you into one specific provider.

What types of projects are a good fit?

Common engagements include performance tuning, replication and HA reviews, designing backup & recovery strategies, planning and executing migrations or upgrades, and ongoing monthly health checks for production systems.

Can you work with our existing developers or DevOps team?

Absolutely. Ofetech often plugs into existing teams, pairing with engineers, SREs, and DevOps staff to design database changes that fit your current tooling and processes.

What does pricing look like?

Pricing depends on scope, urgency, and whether you want a project-based engagement or ongoing support. After your initial review, Ofetech will outline one or more options so you can choose what best matches your needs and budget.

Still wondering if Ofetech is right for you?

Tell us about your databases. If we’re not the right fit, we’ll say so—and suggest alternatives.
